# **Privacy Policy – ENA by Enablerz AI**

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

This Privacy Policy explains how Enablerz AI ("Enablerz AI", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, processes, and protects personal information when you use **ENA**, the enterprise coaching platform developed by Enablerz AI.

ENA enables organizations to provide structured coaching experiences to employees through a combination of certified human coaches and technology-enabled tools.

This policy applies to all users of ENA including enterprise customers, employees, coaches, and administrators.

---

## **1. Information We Collect**

We may collect the following types of information when you use ENA:

### **Account Information**

* Name
* Email address
* Organization details
* Role within the organization
* Login credentials

### **Coaching Session Information**

* Coaching goals and topics
* Session scheduling information
* Progress tracking data
* Coaching feedback and reflections voluntarily provided by users

### **Calendar Data (If Enabled)**

When coaches connect their calendar (such as Google Calendar), ENA may access limited calendar data necessary to manage coaching sessions, including:

* Event title
* Start and end time
* Time zone
* Session scheduling metadata

ENA only accesses calendar information required to schedule and synchronize coaching sessions.

### **Usage Information**

We may automatically collect information about how users interact with ENA, including:

* IP address
* Device information
* Browser type
* Pages visited
* Time spent on the platform

### **Organizational Data**

Enterprise clients may provide employee-related information necessary to deliver coaching services such as:

* Employee role or department
* Leadership level
* Program participation status

---

## **2. How We Use Information**

We use collected information to:

* Deliver coaching services through ENA
* Schedule and manage coaching sessions
* Match employees with coaches
* Improve coaching experiences and platform functionality
* Provide analytics and insights to enterprise customers (in aggregated form)
* Ensure platform security and compliance
* Communicate with users regarding sessions, updates, and platform changes

ENA does **not sell personal information** to third parties.

---

## **3. Data Shared with Organizations**

When ENA is used within an organization, certain information may be shared with the enterprise customer in **aggregated or program-level form**, including:

* Coaching participation rates
* Program engagement metrics
* Organizational insights derived from aggregated coaching data

Individual coaching conversations remain confidential unless users explicitly choose to share them.
