## Google Calendar Access & Integration

When a Coach chooses to connect their Google Calendar with the Enablerz Platform, we request explicit permission through Google's OAuth authorization process to access limited calendar data.

### Data We Access

With your consent, we may access:

- Event title, start time, end time, and timezone;
- Event metadata required for scheduling or updating sessions;
- Calendar identifiers needed for synchronization.

We do not access any data beyond what is required for session scheduling and synchronization.

### Purpose of Access

We use this data only to:

- Sync coaching sessions between Enablerz and your Google Calendar;
- Automatically create, update, or cancel events when sessions are scheduled, rescheduled, or canceled;
- Ensure accurate scheduling for Coaches and Coachees.

### Consent and Control

- Access is granted only after you approve it on Google's consent screen.
- You can revoke access anytime through your Enablerz settings or your Google account permissions.
- If access is revoked, calendar syncing will stop.

### Compliance

Our use of Google Calendar data follows the Google API Services User Data Policy and is limited strictly to the purposes described above.
