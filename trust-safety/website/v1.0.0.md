# **Trust & Safety at Enablerz**

At **Enablerz**, trust, privacy, and responsible technology are central to how we design and operate our coaching platform.

We are committed to protecting user data, maintaining coaching confidentiality, and building AI systems responsibly. This page outlines the principles and practices that guide how Enablerz delivers safe and secure coaching experiences through our products.

The Enablerz platform includes:

**ENA™** – an enterprise coaching platform that enables organizations to deliver coaching through certified human coaches supported by digital tools.

**MOAI™** – an AI-native coaching companion designed to provide scalable coaching guidance and insights.

Enablerz is committed to protecting the privacy of all users of the platform.

## **Coaching Confidentiality**

Coaching conversations are designed to support reflection, development, and wellbeing.

Individual coaching sessions conducted through the platform are treated as confidential interactions between coaches and participants.

Organizations using the platform may receive **aggregated program insights**, but individual coaching conversations remain private unless voluntarily shared by users.

---

## **Responsible AI**

Enablerz is committed to the responsible development and use of artificial intelligence.

AI-powered services such as **MOAI™** are designed to assist users with reflection, guidance, and coaching support.

AI systems:

* are intended to augment coaching experiences, not replace human judgment

* may generate automated responses based on user inputs

* should not be considered professional legal, medical, or psychological advice

We continuously work to improve the safety, transparency, and reliability of AI features.

---

## **Platform Security**

Enablerz implements technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect user data and platform integrity.

Security practices may include:

* secure cloud infrastructure

* encryption of data in transit

* access controls and authentication mechanisms

* monitoring for suspicious activity

While no online service can guarantee absolute security, we take reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access or misuse.
